Mission Statement

Current Progress:

   We are currently in the early stages of development and our current objective is working on deployment of python libraries through the django framework. As of now, we have setup the framework of our application by creating an appropriate environment with Django . We are working through adding in the plotly libraries full functionalities in bringing user inputs to the webpages we've established.

Purpose:

  As students at Georgia Southern currently enrolled in Professor John Repko's Computer Science Capstone, we have been tasked with developing an application for data visualization and comprehension. Under the guidance of Professors Hui and Allen, we hope to appeal to the kinesthetic learner in offering a digital environment for high school student interact with data and graphs.
  Although the current method of teaching someone to understand data isn’t incorrect, we hope to diversify the methods available and appeal to those who’ve struggled with previous styles. There have been programs built like this before; however, the programs have become outdated and clunky compared to modern expectations.
